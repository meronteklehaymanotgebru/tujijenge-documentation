Tujijenge Documentation

Welcome to Tujijenge Documentation.

Tujijenge Documentation provides clear guidance on how vendors, suppliers, and trainers interact within the platform. The system is designed to support small-scale vendors in Kenya by enabling group ordering, inventory management, and access to training opportunities on hygiene and business growth. This documentation outlines the key components, user journeys, and system architecture to help all stakeholders understand and navigate the platform effectively.

Who are our users?

Our users include small-scale vendors (mama mbogas), trusted suppliers, and hygiene and business management trainers. Vendors can join groups around their 1000m radius to order fresh produce, manage inventory, and access training. Suppliers fulfill group orders, while trainers offer sessions on hygiene, business skills, and verification.

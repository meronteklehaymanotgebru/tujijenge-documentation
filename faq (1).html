Tujijenge FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Vendors

Q. How does group ordering work, how do I pay in a group? +

A.Group ordering allows Mama Mbogas to collaborate with other vendors within a 1000m radius to place bulk orders with Taimba suppliers. Here’s how it works:
Individual Orders: You place your order individually through the Tujijenge mobile app, specifying the products and quantities you need.
Group Consolidation: The app groups your order with others in your community (vendors within 1000m). The supplier (Taimba) sees the order as a single bulk order on their web portal, which includes details of each vendor’s contribution. Order Closure: Orders close automatically once the minimum quantity requirement set by Taimba is met.
Payment and Fulfillment: You receive an individual payment request via M-Pesa. Once paid, Taimba processes the bulk order and coordinates delivery or pickup times, ensuring fresher produce at lower costs due to bulk purchasing.

Q. How do I join a vendor group? +

A.To participate in group ordering and use the Tujijenge app, you must be part of a community:
Group ordering allows Mama Mbogas to collaborate with other vendors within a 1000m radius to place bulk orders with Taimba suppliers. Here’s how it works:
Joining a Community: When you register in the app, it uses geolocation to identify existing vendor communities within a 1000m radius of your kiosk. You can browse and join an available community directly in the app.
Creating a Community: If no community exists within 1000m, you can create one through the app. Navigate to the “Community” section, select “Create Community,” and provide a name and description. Other vendors in your area can join your community.
Why Communities Matter: Communities enable group ordering, which reduces costs and ensures efficient order fulfillment. You must be in a community to place orders.

Q. What if my order is delayed? +

A. Contact support through the platform; we’ll coordinate with suppliers to resolve delays promptly.

Suppliers

Q. How do I access the platform as a supplier? +

A. Signin as a supplier in the stakeholder webapp.

Q. How do group orders appear in the Taimba web portal? +

A. Group orders from Mama Mboga communities are consolidated into a single bulk order in the Taimba web portal.
Each order includes detailed breakdowns of individual vendor contributions (e.g., vendor name, products, quantities).
You can view pending orders, update catalogue and view impact.
Once fulfilled, notify the community via the portal, and vendors receive updates in their app.

Q. What are the delivery requirements? +

A. Suppliers must deliver within 12 hours and meet our quality standards outlined in the agreement.

Trainers

Q. How do I sign in to the GAIN web portal? +

A. Access the GAIN web portal via the provided URL. Log in using your GAIN-provided credentials (username and password).

Q. How do I schedule and notify Mama Mbogas about training sessions? +

A. Log in to the GAIN web portal.
Navigate to the “Training” section and select “Create New Session.”
Enter details like date, time, location, and topic (e.g., food safety, financial literacy).
The system automatically notifies Mama Mbogas in the app about upcoming sessions, and they can register directly.
You can also send reminders via the portal or linked WhatsApp groups.

Q.How do I track the impact of training programs? +

A. Use the “Dashboard” section in the GAIN web portal to view engagement statistics, such as the number of Mama Mbogas certified, session attendance rates, and active communities.

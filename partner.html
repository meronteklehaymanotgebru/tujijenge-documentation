Tujijenge Partners

Introduction to Tujijenge Partnerships

Tujijenge Partnerships collaborates with organizations that support small-scale vendors in Kenya, specifically mama mbogas. Currently, our key partners are Taimba and the Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition (GAIN), who help enhance the platform's supply chain and educational support. This documentation outlines their roles and how to engage with us as of July 27, 2025, 12:06 PM EAT.

Our Partners

Our current partners include Taimba and GAIN, who are integral to Tujijenge's mission of empowering vendors through efficient supply chains and nutrition initiatives.

Taimba

Taimba is a Nairobi-based agri-tech company that connects smallholder farmers directly to urban traders, reducing post-harvest losses and ensuring affordable, fresh produce for mama mbogas.

Learn More

Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition (GAIN)

GAIN, a Swiss-based foundation, supports business owners by promoting nutritious food access in Kenya since 2010, including fortification programs and SME support in malnutrition hotspots.
